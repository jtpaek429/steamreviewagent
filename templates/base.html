How we calculate these metrics

Current Steam Rating

Steam's official all-time rating for the game, pulled directly from the Steam store. Reflects all reviews in all languages since launch.

Last 7-Day Steam Rating

Review distribution from up to 500 of the most recent English-language reviews in the analysis window (Mon–Sun UTC). May differ from Steam's own "Recent Reviews" label, which counts all languages.

AI Sentiment & Themes

Up to 500 English-language reviews from the same window are sent to Claude for analysis. Claude identifies recurring themes, classifies each as positive/mixed/negative, and surfaces representative quotes. Themes with clearly opposing camps are split into "Praised" and "Criticized" entries instead of collapsed into a single mixed theme.

Note: analysis is scoped to English-language reviews to reflect the English-speaking audience and manage cost.
